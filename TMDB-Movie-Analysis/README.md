# TMDB Movie Data Analysis

## Project Overview
This project investigates the TMDB movies dataset to discover trends in the film industry. It focuses on how budgets affect revenue, the impact of user ratings on profit, and identifying the most successful movie genres over the decades.

## Key Findings
* **Budget vs. Revenue:** There is a clear positive correlation; higher budgets generally lead to higher revenues. However, it is not a 100% guarantee of commercial success.
* **Ratings vs. Profit:** Most financially successful movies tend to score between 6 and 8 in user ratings (`vote_average`). Movies with low ratings (below 5) rarely achieve huge profits.
* **Genre Popularity:** **Animation** and **Adventure** are the top-earning movie genres on average, while **Documentaries** and **Foreign** films tend to generate the lowest profits.
